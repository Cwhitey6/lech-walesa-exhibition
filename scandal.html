Collaboration Scandal

Accusations and Archives

Beginning in the 1990s, historians and political opponents claimed that Wałęsa had cooperated with the communist security service (SB) under the codename “Bolek” in the early 1970s. He has always denied this.

Archival files, expert reports, and court rulings all form part of the contested record around these accusations.
