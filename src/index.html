Who Was Lech Wałęsa?

From shipyard electrician in Gdańsk to Nobel Prize laureate and president, Lech Wałęsa became a symbol of resistance against communist rule in Eastern Europe.

This exhibition guides you room by room through his personal life, political ascent, the collaboration scandal, and the long shadow of his legacy.
